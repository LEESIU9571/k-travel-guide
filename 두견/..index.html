Getting Around Korea
Subway
Use the subway by tapping in and tapping out at the gate with your T-money card.
Buy a T-money card at convenience stores like CU or GS25, then add money at station machines.
Tip for foreigners: Download Naver Map or Kakao Metro because station names are shown in English.
Bus
Board from the front door, tap your T-money card, and tap again when you get off.
The same T-money card works on city buses, and transfer discounts apply when you tap correctly.
Tip for foreigners: Press the stop bell one stop early so you do not miss your stop.
KTX (High Speed Train)
Use KTX for fast travel between major cities by booking a seat online or at the station.
T-money is not used for KTX tickets, so buy a separate KTX ticket in advance.
Tip for foreigners: Arrive 15 minutes early to find the right platform and car number without stress.
